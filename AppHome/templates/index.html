Our Story

Welcome to Waves & Wings, where coastal freshness meets urban crunch right here in London! Our journey started with a simple, passionate idea: why choose between the finest treasures of the sea and the ultimate comfort of perfectly crisped wings? We set out to create a vibrant kitchen that honors both. Every dish we serve is a celebration of bold flavors, high-quality ingredients, and culinary craftsmanship, designed to bring a wave of excitement to your taste buds with every single bite.

From our sustainably sourced, golden-batter Fish & Seafood to our signature, succulent Chicken Wings tossed in a variety of house-crafted sauces, we refuse to compromise on quality. Whether you are grabbing a quick, satisfying Meal Deal on your lunch break or sharing a loaded fries feast with friends, Waves & Wings is built on a promise of good food and great flavor, every single time. Thank you for welcoming us into the neighborhood—we are proud to be your new local favorite!
